Info

Opens a Popover or Modal based on presentation prop.

<Info
  content={'Your address has been verified, no more actions are required.'}
  presentation={Info.Presentation.POPOVER}
  size={Info.Size.SMALL}
  title={'You entered your address'}
  aria-label="Click here for more details"
/>
You entered your address
Your address has been verified, no more actions are required.
